Laitila App - Last updated 18 May 2026

Laitila App collects useful local information about Laitila into one app. The app is intended for locals, newcomers, visitors, and local businesses.

This public page uses no tracking, no backend, and no cookies.

Information Accuracy

The app may contain errors, missing details, or outdated information. Users should verify important details, opening hours, prices, event times, and official notices from the original source before relying on them.

Corrections And Submissions

Users can submit missing places, events, feedback, bug reports, and corrections. Submissions may be stored in Supabase for admin review before publishing.

Laitila App may edit, combine, reject, or remove submissions to keep the app accurate, safe, and useful.

No Normal User Login

Normal use does not require login.

Saved Places

Saved places are stored locally on the user's device. They are not a cloud backup and may be lost if the app data is removed.

Reminders And Permissions

Event reminders are local device notifications. App notifications are optional and can be disabled.

Location is optional and is used only for nearby, distance, and route features when the user allows it.

Contact

Questions can be sent to laitilaapp@gmail.com.
