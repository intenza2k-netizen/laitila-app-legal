Privacy Policy

Laitila App - Last updated 18 May 2026

Laitila App is a local information app for Laitila, Finland. It shows places, events, alerts, Quick Help, and useful local information from the app database. Normal users do not need an account.

This public page uses no tracking, no backend, and no cookies.

Information Users Choose To Send

Laitila App may store information when a user chooses to submit it, such as feedback, bug reports, correction reports, place updates, missing places, or event suggestions.

  • Submission text provided by the user
  • Optional contact email or phone number if the user provides it
  • Related place or event details when correcting existing content
  • Basic bug report context such as screen name, app version, platform, and timestamp
  • Submission status and admin review notes

Feedback and report submissions may be stored for admin review. Optional contact details are used only to review or follow up on the submitted issue.

App Data Services

Laitila App uses Supabase for app data, submissions, and admin review workflows. The app uses Google Maps and Google Places for map and place data. External place data may be imported or cached before it is shown in the app.

Saved Places And Local Data

Saved places are stored locally on the user's device. They are not synced to Supabase and do not require login. Language choice, onboarding status, notification preferences, and local event reminder ids may also be stored locally on the device.

Location And Notifications

Location is optional. The app asks for location only when the user uses a nearby, distance, map-centering, or route feature that needs it. The app does not use background location tracking.

Event reminders are local notifications on the user's device. Notifications are optional and can be disabled.

No Sale Of Personal Data

Laitila App does not sell personal data. Personal information from submissions is used only to operate, review, improve, and protect the app and its local information.

Corrections And Deletion

Users can request correction or deletion of submitted personal information by contacting laitilaapp@gmail.com.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be sent to laitilaapp@gmail.com.
